Planning a website
  • To achieve higher success of the website in terms of

    user satisfaction, better Planning is needed

  • Before we start developing a website ,we should ask question such as:
    • Why are we developing this website?
    • What do we achieve by developing this website?
    • Who will use this website?
    • What are the information contents?
    • How are these contents organized?
    • What are the possible ways?
